The cost to level is the desired level in character points. If you wish to level a [[skill]] more than one level at a time you must pay for the intermediary levels too.

|Level|Point Cost|Total Point Cost|
|-|-|-|
|1|1|1|
|2|2|3|
|3|3|6|
|4|4|10|
|5|5|15|
|x|x|x(x+1)/2|
