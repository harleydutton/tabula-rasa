## Design Pillars
- plays quickly
- promotes drama
- no source material
- generic
- low barrier to entry
- DRY using links
- avoid complicated math
- minimal book-keeping

### In Scope
- character creation
- rolling checks
- settings
- combat mechanics
- setting creation

### Out of Scope
- story/adventure template
- probability charts
- source material
- forming the PC party
- preachy-ness/tips
