[[Character|Characters]] come to the small mining town of New Prospect via [[Train]]. On the train ride in they should always see a few things, in order:
1. A burnt out town with scorch marks across the tracks.
2. Bandits taking harmless pot-shots at the train with long rifles.
3. A sign declaring "Welcome to New Prospect" with a subtext of "Strike it Rich!"
4. People with plate armor, guns, and boxes of gold and jewels getting onto the train and heading [[Back East]].
5. The town of [[New Prospect]].
