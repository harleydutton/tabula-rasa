[[Character|Characters]] come to the small mining town of New Prospect via [[Train]]. On the train ride in they should always see a few things, in order:
1. A horse and rider fleeing through the [[Hoodoos]]. A black and white bird plucks the rider off the horse and flies into the sun's glare with him.
2. A sign declaring "Welcome to New Prospect" with a subtext of "Strike it Rich!"
3. People with plate armor, guns, and boxes of gold and jewels getting onto the train and heading [[Back East]]. They are very paranoid and haunted looking.
4. A short welcome speech from the [[Mayor]] directing prospectors looking for work to the [[Quartermaster]].
5. The town of [[New Prospect]].
