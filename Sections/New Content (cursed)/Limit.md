A limit is a rule added to [[Content]] in order to balance it. Limits can also be used to help define [[Restriction|Restrictions]]. Examples include:
- [[Charges and Cooldowns]]
- [[Context]]
- [[Power Level]]
- [[Risk]]
- [[Scope (unlucky)]]
- [[Secondary Resource]]
- [[Timing]]
- [[Trade Off (unlucky)]]
- [[Rarity]]
