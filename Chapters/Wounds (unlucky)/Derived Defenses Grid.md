Characters will have defenses derived from the combination of each of their attributes (mind, body, spirit, and social) and either [[Health]] or [[Armor]]. There will be a little grid that gets filled in. The derived stats are the lower of the two in each instance so with 2 social and 3 armor, social armor would be 2.

|           |     | armor | health |
| --------- | --- | ----- | ------ |
|           |     | 2     | 3      |
| physical  | 4   | 2     | 2      |
| mental    | 3   | 2     | 3      |
| spiritual | 2   | 2     | 2      |
| social    | 1   | 1     | 1      |
